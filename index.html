Many fans of “Doki Doki Literature Club!” still want to spend more time with their favorite club members, beyond what DDLC+ can provide. Our mod, Natsulogue, will allow you to spend more time with the best character, Natsuki! At the moment our mod is still under development, but a lot of progress with the dialogue and game mechanics has already been made. Um... Oh, is there someone here? Would you like to talk with me? It’s boring being here all alone, so download these files! Even a dummy can do it. Don't keep me waiting...
